    How To Play

    The goal of SpyFall is to find out who the spy is, or if you're the spy, to stay hidden and find out the secret location. After everyone playing joins the lobby, the game can be started. Once started, the game randomly assigns one player to be the spy, and everyone else is informed of the secret location. One player is chosen to start the questioning. For the non-spies, questioning serves as a way to find out who the spy is and let the others know that you yourself are not the spy. However, the non-spies must be careful not to leak too much information, as at any time, the spy can reveal himself and guess the secret location. If he guesses correctly, the spy wins and the game ends; if he guesses wrongly, the non-spies win. Similarly, at any time, the non-spies can guess who the spy is. If they guess correctly, the non-spies win.
